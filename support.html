Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play Desi Spy?

Desi Spy is a pass-and-play party game for 3-15 players. One phone is passed around, and everyone except the imposter sees a secret Bollywood word. Give one-word clues and try to identify who doesn't know the word!

Do I need WiFi or internet?

No! Desi Spy works completely offline. All you need is one device and your friends.

How many players can play?

The game supports 3-15 players on a single device.

What categories are included?

We have 250+ words across 5 categories: Classic Films (1950s-1990s), Modern Films (2000-2025), Legendary Actors, Iconic Songs, and Memorable Characters.

Is there a cost?

Desi Spy is completely free with no ads, in-app purchases, or subscriptions.

The app crashed or isn't working. What should I do?

Try these steps:

  1. Force close the app and reopen it
  2. Restart your device
  3. Make sure you have the latest version from the App Store
  4. If the problem persists, email us with details about your device and iOS version

Can I suggest new words or features?

Absolutely! We'd love to hear your ideas. Email us at hiren.mandalia+desispy@gmail.com
