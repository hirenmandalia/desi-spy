Desi Spy - Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 15, 2026

Overview

Desi Spy is a local, pass-and-play party game that values your privacy. This app does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Information We Don't Collect

Desi Spy does not collect any of the following:

  • Personal information (names, email addresses, phone numbers)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Photos, contacts, or any other device data

How the App Works

Desi Spy is a completely offline, single-device game. All game data (player counts, roles, words, timer settings) exists only in your device's temporary memory while the app is running. No data is saved, uploaded, or shared with any servers.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app is designed for ages 13+ and contains no data collection mechanisms.

Changes to This Policy

If we make any changes to this privacy policy in future updates, we will update this page and the "Last updated" date above.
